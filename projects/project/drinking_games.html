Flip and Drink

Requirements

Six pack of Karhu
Any number of players


Rules

The Rules are quite simple, even a dead drunk person can follow them.
Just call Head or Tail then hit the flip button.
If the picture turns out to be the one you called, you can
pass on from taking a shot of drink and give your friend a chance
to flip, but if your prediction was wrong then you must take the shot
